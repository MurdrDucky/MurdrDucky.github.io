UNICEF

UNICEF USA and UNICEF are driven by a shared vision: a world that upholds the rights of all children and helps every child thrive. UNICEF works in over 190 countries and territories, helping to save and meaningfully improve the lives of children globally — focusing on the most vulnerable. UNICEF USA advances the global mission of UNICEF by rallying the American public to support the world’s most vulnerable children. UNICEF USA is one of 33 National Committees established to drive impact for the world's children. Visit Products
