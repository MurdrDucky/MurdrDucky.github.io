The Trevor Project

Our long-term vision is to build a world where we are no longer needed — where every LGBTQ+ young person is loved and accepted for who they are, and therefore not at higher risk for suicide or mental health crisis. Until then, we’ll continue to meet the need sustainably and refine our efforts intentionally so that we can provide support to every LGBTQ+ young person who needs it. Visit Products
